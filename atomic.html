ATOMIC WINTER

DYSTOPIA PHASE I: ATOMIC WINTER is a project I did for LaMode Fashion Club
as the Video Director and photographer. The project depicts the aftermaths
of a massive nulcear bomb explosion - a scenario called ‘Atomic Winter’.

Nuclear bomb has always been a pride in military weapons,
but the destruction it brings to humanity is
fatal: potential extinction of human and complete erosion of the ecology.
