SERPENTINE WISDOM

Serpentine Wisdom," a personal project where I served as
Creative Director and Photographer, reflects my dedication to Vietnamese beauty
and culture. The project showcases the traditional "ao yem" and explores the concept
of eternity. My friend contributed as the costume designer.
It has been featured on Photo Vogue.
